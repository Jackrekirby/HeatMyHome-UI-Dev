Motivation

As part of the UK governmentâ€™s commitment to achieving net-zero emissions by 2050, the installation of gas boilers in new homes will be banned from 2025. Furthermore, the government expects to phase out the sale of gas boilers to existing homes by 2035, through the offering of grants and incentives to homeowners for switching to low-carbon heating appliances. Therefore, there is a need to educate homeowners about the several alternative low-carbon heating solutions available to encourage them to switch, whilst making sure they select an appliance that will meet their requirements.

We wanted to create a website that would make it as easy and as quick as possible to do this. By using HeatMyHome, homeowners are able to input details about their home as parameters to simulate these different low-carbon heating solutions to make an informed decision about which alternative is most suitable for their home.
